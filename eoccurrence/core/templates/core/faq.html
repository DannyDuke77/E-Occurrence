What is e-Occurrence?

View Details Hide Details
e-Occurrence is a digital platform for police, courts, and the public to record, track, and manage criminal cases. It replaces manual occurrence books with a secure and transparent system.

Who can use e-Occurrence?

View Details Hide Details
  • Police officers - record OB entries, bail, and case updates.
  • Court officials - update hearings, rulings, and case status.
  • Public users - view limited case information for transparency.

How can the public check case information?

View Details Hide Details
  1. Visit the e-Occurrence public portal.
  2. Enter the case reference number provided by police/court.
  3. View the latest updates (hearing dates, rulings, bail status, etc.).
  4. Note: sensitive information remains restricted to authorized officials.

Who can see sensitive case information?

View Details Hide Details
Sensitive information in e-Occurrence is strictly restricted to authorized personnel. Police officers and court officials have access to full case details, including confidential information, while public users see only general updates like hearing dates, case status, or bail information. Every access and view of sensitive data is logged to maintain accountability, transparency, and compliance with data protection regulations.
